24-Hour Circles

This simple, circular clock has four wheels:
The outermost displays the hours passed in the day,
the next shows the minutes per hour, the third shows
the seconds and the innermost completes once
every second.
The current date and time and diplayed in the centre.

Coded on the HTML5 canvas element, this clock's
functionality is programmed entirely in javacsript.

24-Hour Bars

This clock uses bar-chart style graphics
to show the time. The top bar show the number of
hours passed in the day, the next shows the minutes
per hour and the third shows the seconds per
minute.
Below sit simple wheel icons to show the progress
through the days of the week, the date of the month,
the month of the year and, finally, the year of
the century.
